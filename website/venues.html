Common questions

What does the $499 cover? +

The full Tournament Pass: your venue listed on every relevant match-detail page, unlimited specials and menu updates, host-city guide placement, atmosphere settings, watch-party hosting, and analytics — from the day you sign up through the final on July 19, 2026.

Do you take a cut of my sales? +

No. We don't process your transactions, take commissions on check-ins, or charge per booking. $499 one-time is the whole bill.

I own multiple venues. Can I list all of them? +

Yes — one account can claim up to 5 venues. Each venue purchases its own Tournament Pass. Reach out if you need to list more than 5.

How do you verify ownership? +

We send a verification code via SMS to the business phone number on file. The code expires in 10 minutes. If you have trouble, our support team can verify manually with a business license.

What if my venue isn't already in the app? +

You can add it during signup. As soon as you complete verification and payment, your venue goes live in the app and starts appearing in fan searches near host cities.

What happens after the tournament ends? +

Your venue stays in our directory at no cost. We'll be in touch about post-tournament tiers as Pregame expands to other leagues — but there's no obligation to continue.

How do I get help? +

Email support@pregameworldcup.com any time. We typically respond within one business day.
