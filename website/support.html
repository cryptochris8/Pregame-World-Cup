Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store. You can sign up using your email address or sign in with your Google account for quick access.

Are the AI predictions accurate?

Our AI predictions are designed for entertainment purposes and to enhance your World Cup experience. While we use advanced algorithms and historical data, sports outcomes are inherently unpredictable. Please do not use our predictions for gambling or betting decisions.

How do I find watch parties near me?

Enable location services in the app to discover nearby venues and watch parties. You can filter by distance, atmosphere, and match schedule to find the perfect spot to watch the games.

Is the app free to use?

Yes! The core features of Pregame World Cup are free. We offer an optional Fan Pass Premium subscription for advanced AI predictions, exclusive content, and an ad-free experience.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing us at support@pregameworldcup.com. We will process your request and delete all associated data within 30 days.

Which devices are supported?

Pregame World Cup is available for iOS devices running iOS 14.0 or later, and Android devices running Android 6.0 (API level 23) or later.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We love hearing from our users! Send us an email at support@pregameworldcup.com with details about the bug you encountered or the feature you'd like to see. Please include your device type and app version to help us investigate.
