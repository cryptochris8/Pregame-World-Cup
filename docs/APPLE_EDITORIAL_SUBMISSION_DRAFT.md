## How to Submit

### Step 1: Access App Store Connect
1. Go to [appstoreconnect.apple.com](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple Developer account
3. Click on your app
4. In the left sidebar, find **"Featuring Nominations"** (under Marketing)

### Step 2: Create Nomination
1. Click **"Create Nomination"** or **"+"**
2. Select **"App Launch"** as the nomination type

### Step 3: Fill Out Form
1. Enter the basic information from the table above
2. Copy/paste the Primary Description
3. Copy/paste the Helpful Details (remember to personalize the Founder Story)
4. Add all your supporting URLs

### Step 4: Submit
1. Review everything for typos
2. Click **"Submit"**
3. Take a screenshot for your records

---

## After Submission

### Timeline
- Apple may take up to 30 days to review
- If selected, you'll receive a push notification
- If not selected, you won't be notified (this is normal)

### If Selected
1. Access "Promote Your App" marketing assets in App Store Connect
2. Download official "Featured on the App Store" badges
3. Update your press kit with featuring status
4. Share on social media
5. Reach out to media contacts with the news

### If Not Selected
- You can submit again for "App Enhancement" when you ship major updates
- Focus on other marketing channels
- This doesn't reflect on your app's quality - Apple features a small percentage

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Submit Early** - Don't wait until launch day. Apple needs lead time to plan.

2. **Keep TestFlight Updated** - Apple's editors may download and test your app.

3. **Emphasize Innovation** - Your dual AI integration is genuinely novel. Lead with this.

4. **Accessibility Matters** - Apple deeply values accessibility. You have strong features here.

5. **Event Timing** - The World Cup timing is your biggest asset. Emphasize this heavily.

6. **Quality Over Everything** - One well-crafted nomination beats multiple rushed ones.

## Video Creation Tips

### App Preview Video (for App Store listing)
- 15-30 seconds
- Screen recording of actual app
- No hands/bezels - just the app
- First 3 seconds are critical (hook viewers)
- Add captions (80% watch without sound)
- Highlight: AI predictions, watch parties, social features

### Promo Video (for nomination)
- 30-60 seconds
- Can be more marketing-focused
- Show the value proposition
- Upload to YouTube/Vimeo for a shareable URL

### Tools
- **Free:** iPhone screen recording + iMovie/CapCut
- **Paid:** ScreenFlow, Final Cut Pro
