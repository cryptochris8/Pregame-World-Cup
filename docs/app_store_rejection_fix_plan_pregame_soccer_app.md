# 📱 App Store Rejection Fix Plan
## Pregame App (Soccer / 2026 Tournament)

---

## 🚨 Overview
This document outlines the required fixes to resolve App Store rejection issues related to:

1. **Intellectual Property (FIFA-related)**
2. **Typography / Readability Issues**

These updates are designed to ensure compliance with Apple App Store guidelines and improve approval chances on resubmission.

---

# ⚖️ 1. Intellectual Property Fix (Guideline 5.2.1)

## ❗ Problem
Apple flagged the app for resembling FIFA-related branding without authorization.

## 🎯 Goal
Remove any implication of official affiliation with FIFA or the World Cup.

---

## ✅ Required Changes

### 1. App Name Update (CRITICAL)
Avoid using "World Cup" in a way that suggests official affiliation.

### 🔁 Recommended Alternatives:
- Pregame: Soccer Fan Guide 2026
- Pregame: Global Soccer Companion
- Pregame: 2026 Soccer Fan App
- Pregame: International Soccer Guide

---

### 2. Legal Disclaimer (REQUIRED)
Add this disclaimer in BOTH:
- App Store description
- In-app (settings page or footer)

```text
Pregame is an independent fan app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FIFA or any official tournament organization.
```

---

### 3. Remove or Adjust Risky Content
Audit the app and REMOVE or MODIFY:

- FIFA logos or similar designs
- World Cup trophy imagery
- Official tournament branding styles
- UI that resembles FIFA apps or website

---

### 4. Metadata Cleanup

#### ❌ Avoid:
- "Official World Cup app"
- "World Cup 2026 companion"
- "Follow the World Cup"

#### ✅ Replace With:
- "Global soccer tournament"
- "International soccer event"
- "Soccer fans traveling in 2026"

---

## 💡 Positioning Strategy (Recommended)

Instead of:
> "World Cup App"

Use:
> "Fan companion app for international soccer events"

---

# 🎨 2. Typography Fix (Guideline 4)

## ❗ Problem
Apple identified hard-to-read typography.

---

## ✅ Required UI Improvements

### 1. Font Size
- Minimum body text: **16pt**
- Headings: 18–24pt+

---

### 2. Contrast

Ensure strong contrast:

#### ✅ Good:
- Dark text on white background
- White text on dark background

#### ❌ Avoid:
- Light gray on white
- Neon colors on light backgrounds
- Thin/light font weights

---

### 3. Text Over Images Fix

If using background images:

#### ✅ Solution:
- Add a **dark overlay (40–60% opacity)**
- Use **white text on top**

---

### 4. Font Style

#### ✅ Use:
- System fonts (San Francisco / iOS default)
- Clean, readable sans-serif fonts

#### ❌ Avoid:
- Script fonts
- Decorative fonts
- Overly condensed fonts

---

### 5. Spacing

- Increase line height (1.3–1.5x)
- Add padding around text blocks
- Avoid cramped UI

---

```text
2. Typography:
- Improved text readability throughout the app by increasing font sizes.

Thank you for your time and consideration.
```
